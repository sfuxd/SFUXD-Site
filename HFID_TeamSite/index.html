Welcome

Hello! We are team Fallera, and we are exploring a social cooking app for our project in the fall of 2012 semester of Human Factors and Interface Design (HFID) class at Olin. We hope to apply what we learned as sophomores in User-Oriented Collaborative Design (UOCD) by designing user-oriented interfaces for software. Our goal is to make social cooking fun and easy by designing an app that allows users to quickly and easily schedule cooking events with friends.
