Chocolate Swirl Strawberries
Cook time: 15 mins
4 c strawberries
5 skewers
2 c chocolate chips
1 T lemon juice
1. Cut stems out of strawberries and toss the strawberries with lemon juice to maintain color.
2. Melt chocolate chips over low heat.
3. While chocolate is melting, stick three strawberries on each skewer.
4. Drizzle chocolate over strawberries and allow to cool at room temperature for 5 minutes, then refrigerate until serving.
